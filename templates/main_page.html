What to do

Fill in the boxes below and click submit. You should be emailed a link to view (and import) your calendar within a few minutes.

You can leave the email field blank and it will be sent to your uni email. However, using your email linked with your existing google calendar can make things easier.

About

This project is made by Tom Moses (a first year Comp Sci student at UoB) and is open source. If you need to contact me, email me.

If you are worries about entering your details into this site, please rest assured that your password is never stored. Your email is temporarily stored while the processing is happening. Your username is stored so that I am able to track statistics of how many users I have.

If you do not want any information stored, there is this very old version I made (which is much more complicated to use).
